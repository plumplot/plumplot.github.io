Cambridgeshire property prices

The average property price in Cambridgeshire county is £309k. The average price increased by £7.9k (3%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £302k. The price of a newly built property is £345k. There were 12.8k property sales and sales dropped by 8.9% (-1.3k transactions). Most properties were sold in the £300k-£400k price range with 2336 (18.2%) properties sold, followed by £200k-£250k price range with 2263 (17.7%) properties sold.
