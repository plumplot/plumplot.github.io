London other crime statistics

Annual crime rate in London region is 1.2, i.e. 1.2 crimes reported per annum per 1000 workday people. Compared to the national crime rate, London's crime rate is at 71%. Other crime makes up 0.9% of all crimes reported in the region. The total number of "other crime" is 10.6k, and this number has decreased by 0.9% when compared year-over-year in the period of March 2019 - February 2020.
