South East London drugs crime statistics

Annual crime rate in South East London postcode area is 6.1, i.e. 6.1 crimes committed per annum per 1000 workday people. Compared to the national crime rate, South East London's crime rate is at 216%. Drugs crime makes up 4.1% of all crimes committed in the postcode area. The total number of "drugs crime" is 5.7k, and this number has increased by 19.8% when compared year-over-year in the period of September 2018 - August 2019.
