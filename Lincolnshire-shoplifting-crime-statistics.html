Lincolnshire shoplifting crime statistics

Annual crime rate in Lincolnshire county is 10 crimes per 1000 people. Compared to the national crime rate, Lincolnshire's rate is at 115% as of August 2025.Shoplifting crime makes up 9.4% of all crimes reported in the county.
