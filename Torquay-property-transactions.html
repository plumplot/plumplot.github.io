Torquay property transactions

There were 4.4k property sales in Torquay postcode area in the previous twelve months and sales dropped by 40.2% (-3.2k transactions). 62 properties, 1.4% were sales of a newly built property. Most properties were sold in the £300k-£400k price range with 824 (18.8%) properties sold, followed by £200k-£250k price range with 723 (16.5%) properties sold.
