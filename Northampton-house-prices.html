Northampton property prices

The average property price in Northampton postcode area is £247k. The average price increased by £243 (0%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £239k. The price of a newly built property is £300k. There were 11.2k property sales and sales dropped by 13.7% (-1.9k transactions). Most properties were sold in the £150k-£200k price range with 2884 (25.7%) properties sold, followed by £200k-£250k price range with 2404 (21.4%) properties sold.
