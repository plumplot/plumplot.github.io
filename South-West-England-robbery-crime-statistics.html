South West England robbery crime statistics

Robbery crime rate is 37% of national crime rate and makes 0.5% of all crimes committed in the region. Annual crime rate is 0.4, i.e. 0.4 crimes committed per annum per 1000 workday people. The total volume of robbery crime is 2.3k and has increased by 17.8% when compared year-over-year in the period of October 2016 - September 2017.
