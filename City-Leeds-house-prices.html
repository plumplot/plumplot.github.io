Leeds property prices

The average property price in Leeds city is £181k. The average price increased by £5.1k (3%) over the last twelve months. The price of a newly built property was 205k and the price of an established property was 179k. There were 7.0k property sales and property sales dropped by 1.3% (-97 sales). Most properties were sold in the £100k-£150k price range with 1890 (27.2%) properties sold, followed by £150k-£200k price range with 1583 (22.7%) properties sold.
