Derbyshire property prices

The average property price in Derbyshire county is £247k, the median price is £210k. The average price increased by £15.7k (7%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £245k. The price of a newly built property is £294k. There were 12.9k property sales and sales dropped by 31.0% (-6.4k transactions). Most properties were sold in the £150k-£200k price range with 2992 (23.1%) properties sold, followed by £100k-£150k price range with 2376 (18.4%) properties sold.

Derbyshire house price to earnings ratio
