Gwynedd newly built property prices

The price of a newly built property is £250k in Gwynedd county. The price increased by £14.2k (6%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £233k. There were 9 sales of newly build properties. Most new properties were sold in the £300k-£400k price range with 3 (33.3%) properties sold, followed by £150k-£200k price range with 3 (33.3%) properties sold. Most new homes were sold in LL77 7, with 3 sold to date from February 2021 - January 2022.
