Berkshire newly built property prices

The average price of newly build property in Berkshire county is £423k. The price increased by £15.5k (4%) over the last twelve months. The price of a newly built property was 423k and the price of an established property was 425k. There were 1911 sales of newly build properties. Most new properties were sold in the £400k-£500k price range with 478 (25.0%) properties sold, followed by £300k-£400k price range with 428 (22.4%) properties sold. Most new homes were sold in RG2 9, with 188 sold to date from September 2018 - August 2019.
