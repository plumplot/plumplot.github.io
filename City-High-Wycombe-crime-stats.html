High Wycombe crime stats

The overall crime rate in High Wycombe city is 115.6 crimes per 1,000. Most crimes, 3.5k crimes were violent crimes which is 34.8% of all crimes committed in the area. Violent crime rate is at 115% of national crime rate. Shoplifting crime was the fastest growing crime and it increased by 58.8% over the last twelve months.The category of "vehicle crime" is the highest ranking crime category when category crime rate is compared to the national average. "Vehicle crime" rate is at 165% of national crime rate which puts High Wycombe on 14. position out of 99 England's and Wales' cities in this crime category. "Vehicle crime" makes up 8.7% of all crimes committed in the area. The total number of "vehicle crime" is 868 cases and has increased by 11.3% when compared year-over-year in the period of December 2023 - November 2024.
