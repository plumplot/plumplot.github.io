Bolton property transactions

There were 5.5k property sales in Bolton postcode area in the previous twelve months and sales dropped by 4.1% (-251 transactions). 187 properties, 3.4% were sales of a newly built property. Most properties were sold in the £100k-£150k price range with 1592 (29.1%) properties sold, followed by £50k-£100k price range with 1355 (24.8%) properties sold.
