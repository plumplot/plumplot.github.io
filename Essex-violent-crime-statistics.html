Essex violent crime statistics

Annual crime rate in Essex county is 42.9, i.e. 42.9 crimes reported per annum per 1000 workday people. Compared to the national crime rate, Essex's crime rate is at 135%. Violent crime makes up 33.0% of all crimes reported in the county. The total number of "violent crime" is 69.1k, and this number has increased by 20.2% when compared year-over-year in the period of January 2019 - December 2019.
