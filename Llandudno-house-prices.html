Llandudno property prices

The average property price in Llandudno postcode area is £230k, the median price is £190k. The average price increased by £7.6k (3%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £230k. The price of a newly built property is £249k. There were 6.2k property sales and sales dropped by 30.2% (-3.0k transactions). Most properties were sold in the £150k-£200k price range with 1516 (24.4%) properties sold, followed by £100k-£150k price range with 1409 (22.7%) properties sold.
