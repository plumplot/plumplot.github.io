South Glamorgan property prices

The average property price in South Glamorgan county is £239k. The average price increased by £4.4k (2%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £234k. The price of a newly built property is £281k. There were 7.5k property sales and sales dropped by 6.9% (-593 transactions). Most properties were sold in the £150k-£200k price range with 1856 (24.9%) properties sold, followed by £100k-£150k price range with 1459 (19.6%) properties sold.
