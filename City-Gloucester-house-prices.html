Gloucester property prices

The average property price in Gloucester city is £265k, the median price is £250k. The average price declined by £-3.7k (-1%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £264k. The price of a newly built property is £332k. There were 1.7k property sales and sales dropped by 21.8% (-512 transactions). Most properties were sold in the £200k-£250k price range with 377 (22.2%) properties sold, followed by £250k-£300k price range with 340 (20.0%) properties sold.
