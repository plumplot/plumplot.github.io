Oxfordshire property prices

The average property price in Oxfordshire county is £403k. The average price declined by £-7.8k (-2%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £405k. The price of a newly built property is £395k. There were 9.4k property sales and sales dropped by 11.2% (-1.3k transactions). Most properties were sold in the £300k-£400k price range with 2743 (29.3%) properties sold, followed by £250k-£300k price range with 1518 (16.2%) properties sold.
