Northamptonshire crime stats

September 2018 - August 2019

Most crimes, 23.2k crimes were violent crimes which is 29.0% of all crimes committed in the area. Violent crime rate is at 107% of national crime rate. Public order crime was the fastest growing crime and it increased by 40.0% over the last twelve months.
The category of "vehicle crime" is the highest ranking crime category when category crime rate is compared to the national average. "Vehicle crime" rate is at 136% of national crime rate which puts Northamptonshire on 4. position out of 55 England's and Wales' counties in this crime category. "Vehicle crime" makes up 9.3% of all crimes committed in the area. The total number of "vehicle crime" is 7.4k cases and has increased by 2.5% when compared year-over-year in the period of September 2018 - August 2019.
