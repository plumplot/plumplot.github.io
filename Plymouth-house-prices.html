Plymouth property prices

The average property price in Plymouth postcode area is £279k, the median price is £240k. The average price declined by £-17.7k (-6%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £278k. The price of a newly built property is £325k. There were 6.4k property sales and sales dropped by 18.4% (-1.5k transactions). Most properties were sold in the £200k-£250k price range with 1316 (20.6%) properties sold, followed by £150k-£200k price range with 1262 (19.7%) properties sold.
