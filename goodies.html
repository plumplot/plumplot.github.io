Brighton property prices up by 9% in mid-2017

From July 2016 till June 2017, the property prices in Brighton rose on average by 9% which translates to £29k. In order to buy an average property in Brighton, you would need to pay around £336k. For comparison, the average prices in the whole England and Wales rose by 2% or £6.5k.
