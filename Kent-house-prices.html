Kent property prices

The average property price in Kent county is £323k. The average price increased by £3.8k (1%) over the last twelve months. The price of a newly built property was 364k and the price of an established property was 319k. There were 26.6k property sales and property sales dropped by 11.4% (-3.7k sales). Most properties were sold in the £300k-£400k price range with 5872 (22.1%) properties sold, followed by £200k-£250k price range with 5073 (19.1%) properties sold.
