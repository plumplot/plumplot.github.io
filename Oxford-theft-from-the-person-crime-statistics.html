Oxford theft from the person crime statistics

Annual crime rate in Oxford postcode area is 1.8 crimes per annum per 1000 workday people as of October 2023. Compared to the national crime rate, Oxford's crime rate is at 93%. Theft from the person crime makes up 2.2% of all crimes reported in the postcode area. The total number of "theft from the person crime" is 1.1k, and this number has increased by 30.8% when compared year-over-year in the period of October 2022 - September 2023.
