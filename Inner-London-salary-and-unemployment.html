Inner London net household income map

Map shows net average household income in 2016 by middle layer super output area (MSOA). The lowest household income was in Enfield 030, with £27.8k. The area with the highest average income was Tower Hamlets 033, with £93.8k. Median income was £38.1k - i.e. half of the areas had income equal or above £38.1k. Median income for England and Wales was 31.9k ( the lowest income was in Leeds 086, £16.8k, the highest in Tower Hamlets 033, £93.8k).
