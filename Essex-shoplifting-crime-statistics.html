Essex shoplifting crime statistics

Annual crime rate in Essex county is 6.7, i.e. 6.7 crimes committed per annum per 1000 workday people. Compared to the national crime rate, Essex's crime rate is at 108%. Shoplifting crime makes up 5.2% of all crimes committed in the county. The total number of "shoplifting crime" is 10.9k, and this number has increased by 6.6% when compared year-over-year in the period of September 2018 - August 2019.
