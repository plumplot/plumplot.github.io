Liverpool drugs crime statistics

Annual crime rate in Liverpool postcode area is 8.9, i.e. 8.9 crimes committed per annum per 1000 workday people. Compared to the national crime rate, Liverpool's crime rate is at 318%. Drugs crime makes up 7.1% of all crimes committed in the postcode area. The total number of "drugs crime" is 7.8k, and this number has increased by 37.4% when compared year-over-year in the period of September 2018 - August 2019.
