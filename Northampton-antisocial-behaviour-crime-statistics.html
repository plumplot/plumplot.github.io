Northampton antisocial behaviour crime statistics

Annual crime rate in Northampton postcode area is 26.1, i.e. 26.1 crimes reported per annum per 1000 workday people. Compared to the national crime rate, Northampton's crime rate is at 117%. Antisocial behaviour crime makes up 21.4% of all crimes reported in the postcode area. The total number of "antisocial behaviour crime" is 16.6k, and this number has decreased by 18.8% when compared year-over-year in the period of April 2019 - March 2020.
