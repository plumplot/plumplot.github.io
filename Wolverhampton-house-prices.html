Wolverhampton property prices

The average property price in Wolverhampton postcode area is £220k. The average price increased by £20.6k (10%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £220k. The price of a newly built property is £233k. There were 3.7k property sales and sales dropped by 5.6% (-245 transactions). Most properties were sold in the £150k-£200k price range with 968 (26.3%) properties sold, followed by £100k-£150k price range with 811 (22.1%) properties sold.

Wolverhampton house price to earnings ratio

The median property price to median earnings ratio is ranging between 6.2 in Wolverhampton and 7.4 in Shropshire. Ie. Wolverhampton resident with middle-income needs 6.2 gross annual salaries to buy a medium-priced property. Resident in Shropshire needs 7.4 annual salaries. The England and Wales ratio was 7.69 in 2020.
