Bradford property prices

The average property price in Bradford city is £146k, the median price is £127k. The average price increased by £6.2k (4%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £143k. The price of a newly built property is £209k. There were 3.2k property sales and sales increased by 0.0% (0 transactions). Most properties were sold in the £100k-£150k price range with 1026 (31.8%) properties sold, followed by £50k-£100k price range with 895 (27.8%) properties sold.
