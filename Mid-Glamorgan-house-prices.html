Mid Glamorgan property prices

The average property price in Mid Glamorgan county is £137k. The average price declined by £-1.5k (-1%) over the last twelve months. The price of a newly built property was 195k and the price of an established property was 134k. There were 6.6k property sales and property sales increased by 0.3% (23 sales). Most properties were sold in the £50k-£100k price range with 2176 (32.8%) properties sold, followed by £100k-£150k price range with 1693 (25.5%) properties sold.
