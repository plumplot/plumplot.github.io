Surrey property prices

The average property price in Surrey county is £533k. The average price increased by £6.0k (1%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £537k. The price of a newly built property is £497k. There were 16.8k property sales and sales dropped by 6.4% (-1.2k transactions). Most properties were sold in the £300k-£400k price range with 3981 (23.8%) properties sold, followed by £500k-£750k price range with 3780 (22.6%) properties sold.
