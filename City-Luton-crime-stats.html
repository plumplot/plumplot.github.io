Luton crime stats

The overall crime rate in Luton city is 111.1 crimes per 1,000. Most crimes, 7.7k crimes were violent crimes which is 33.2% of all crimes committed in the area. Violent crime rate is at 104% of national crime rate. Other crime was the fastest growing crime and it increased by 29.8% over the last twelve months.The category of "vehicle crime" is the highest ranking crime category when category crime rate is compared to the national average. "Vehicle crime" rate is at 174% of national crime rate which puts Luton on 8. position out of 99 England's and Wales' cities in this crime category. "Vehicle crime" makes up 8.3% of all crimes committed in the area. The total number of "vehicle crime" is 1.9k cases and has decreased by 1% when compared year-over-year in the period of October 2024 - September 2025.
