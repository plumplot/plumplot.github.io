Buckinghamshire property prices

The average property price in Buckinghamshire county is £484k, the median price is £403k. The average price declined by £-1.6k (0%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £481k. The price of a newly built property is £541k. There were 9.2k property sales and sales dropped by 13.2% (-1.5k transactions). Most properties were sold in the £300k-£400k price range with 2089 (22.6%) properties sold, followed by £500k-£750k price range with 1878 (20.4%) properties sold.
