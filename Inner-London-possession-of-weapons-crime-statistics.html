Inner London possession of weapons crime statistics

Annual crime rate in Inner London postcode area is 0.9, i.e. 0.9 crimes committed per annum per 1000 workday people. Compared to the national crime rate, Inner London's crime rate is at 118%. Possession of weapons crime makes up 0.7% of all crimes committed in the postcode area. The total number of "possession of weapons crime" is 5.2k, and this number has increased by 4% when compared year-over-year in the period of February 2018 - January 2019.
