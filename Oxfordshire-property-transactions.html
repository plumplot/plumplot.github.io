Oxfordshire property transactions

There were 8.1k property sales in Oxfordshire county in the previous twelve months and sales dropped by 14.0% (-1.4k transactions). 597 properties, 7.3% were sales of a newly built property. Most properties were sold in the £300k-£400k price range with 2193 (27.0%) properties sold, followed by £400k-£500k price range with 1632 (20.1%) properties sold.
