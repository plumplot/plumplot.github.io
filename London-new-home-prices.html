London newly built property prices

The price of a newly built property is £648k in London region. The price declined by £-47.9k (-7%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £610k. There were 12239 sales of newly build properties. Most new properties were sold in the £500k-£750k price range with 4082 (33.4%) properties sold, followed by £400k-£500k price range with 2653 (21.7%) properties sold. Most new homes were sold in E20 1, with 703 sold to date from January 2019 - December 2019.
