Merseyside property prices

The average property price in Merseyside county is £164k. The average price increased by £2.6k (2%) over the last twelve months. The price of a newly built property was 167k and the price of an established property was 164k. There were 21.4k property sales and property sales dropped by 5.2% (-1.2k sales). Most properties were sold in the £50k-£100k price range with 5628 (26.3%) properties sold, followed by £100k-£150k price range with 5455 (25.5%) properties sold.
