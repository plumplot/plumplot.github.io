Outer London property prices

The average property price in Outer London city is £496k. The average price increased by £6.5k (1%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £500k. The price of a newly built property is £463k. There were 51.9k property sales and sales dropped by 9.0% (-5.4k transactions). Most properties were sold in the £300k-£400k price range with 13123 (25.3%) properties sold, followed by £500k-£750k price range with 11325 (21.8%) properties sold.
