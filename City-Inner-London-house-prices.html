Inner London property prices

The average property price in Inner London city is £817k. The average price increased by £16.1k (2%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £812k. The price of a newly built property is £838k. There were 34.4k property sales and sales dropped by 11.8% (-4.9k transactions). Most properties were sold in the £500k-£750k price range with 9250 (26.9%) properties sold, followed by over £1M price range with 6223 (18.1%) properties sold.
