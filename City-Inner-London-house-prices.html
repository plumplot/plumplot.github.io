Inner London property prices

The average property price in Inner London city is £794k. The average price declined by £-30.4k (-4%) over the last twelve months. The price of a newly built property was 847k and the price of an established property was 781k. There were 33.7k property sales and property sales dropped by 14.4% (-6.1k sales). Most properties were sold in the £500k-£750k price range with 8838 (26.2%) properties sold, followed by £400k-£500k price range with 6417 (19.1%) properties sold.
