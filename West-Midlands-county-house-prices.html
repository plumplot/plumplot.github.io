West Midlands county property prices

The average property price in West Midlands county county is £207k. The average price increased by £3.2k (2%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £205k. The price of a newly built property is £236k. There were 31.6k property sales and sales dropped by 10.4% (-4.0k transactions). Most properties were sold in the £150k-£200k price range with 8443 (26.7%) properties sold, followed by £100k-£150k price range with 8343 (26.4%) properties sold.
