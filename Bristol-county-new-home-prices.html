Bristol county newly built property prices

The price of a newly built property is £365k in Bristol county county. The price increased by £1.2k (0%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £391k. There were 98 sales of newly build properties. Most new properties were sold in the £300k-£400k price range with 37 (37.8%) properties sold, followed by £400k-£500k price range with 15 (15.3%) properties sold. Most new homes were sold in BS10 7, with 35 sold to date from July 2024 - June 2025.
