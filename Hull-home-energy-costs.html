Hull home energy costs

The following charts compare home energy costs (e.g. heating cost per year, hot water cost per year, heating cost per habitable room, ...) in Hull postcode area to the home energy costs in whole England and Wales. Data is taken from energy certificates for domestic buildings. The certificates were issued between 2008-2019. Data is available for more than 15 million properties.These charts include both Hull house energy costs and Hull flat energy costs.
