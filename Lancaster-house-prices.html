Lancaster property prices

The average property price in Lancaster postcode area is £205k. The average price increased by £9.3k (5%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £201k. The price of a newly built property is £288k. There were 5.5k property sales and sales dropped by 7.9% (-511 transactions). Most properties were sold in the £100k-£150k price range with 1315 (24.0%) properties sold, followed by £150k-£200k price range with 1103 (20.1%) properties sold.
