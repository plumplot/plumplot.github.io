London property prices

The average property price in London region is £686k, the median price is £500k. The average price increased by £31.9k (5%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £683k. The price of a newly built property is £747k. There were 79.9k property sales and sales dropped by 17.3% (-18.2k transactions). Most properties were sold in the £500k-£750k price range with 20861 (26.1%) properties sold, followed by £400k-£500k price range with 15349 (19.2%) properties sold.
