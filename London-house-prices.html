London property prices

The average property price in London region is £678k, the median price is £505k. The average price declined by £-40.0k (-6%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £675k. The price of a newly built property is £996k. There were 64.8k property sales and sales dropped by 24.2% (-22.3k transactions). Most properties were sold in the £500k-£750k price range with 17834 (27.5%) properties sold, followed by £400k-£500k price range with 12687 (19.6%) properties sold.
