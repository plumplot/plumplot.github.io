London property prices

The average property price in London region is £662k. The average price increased by £21.9k (3%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £661k. The price of a newly built property is £677k. There were 79.5k property sales and sales dropped by 1.6% (-1.4k transactions). Most properties were sold in the £500k-£750k price range with 21845 (27.5%) properties sold, followed by £400k-£500k price range with 16495 (20.7%) properties sold.
