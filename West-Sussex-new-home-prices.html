West Sussex newly built property prices

The price of a newly built property is £387k in West Sussex county. The price increased by £4.4k (1%) over the last twelve months. The price of an established property is £371k. There were 1819 sales of newly build properties. Most new properties were sold in the £300k-£400k price range with 585 (32.2%) properties sold, followed by £400k-£500k price range with 380 (20.9%) properties sold. Most new homes were sold in RH12 1, with 133 sold to date from January 2019 - December 2019.
