Rochester other theft crime statistics

Annual crime rate in Rochester postcode area is 10.2, i.e. 10.2 crimes reported per annum per 1000 workday people. Compared to the national crime rate, Rochester's crime rate is at 116%. Other theft crime makes up 7.1% of all crimes reported in the postcode area. The total number of "other theft crime" is 5.8k, and this number has increased by 0.9% when compared year-over-year in the period of January 2019 - December 2019.
