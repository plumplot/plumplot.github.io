Derby property prices

The average property price in Derby postcode area is £208k. The average price increased by £7.1k (4%) over the last twelve months. The price of a newly built property was 268k and the price of an established property was 197k. There were 12.4k property sales and property sales dropped by 5.5% (-786 sales). Most properties were sold in the £100k-£150k price range with 2980 (24.0%) properties sold, followed by £150k-£200k price range with 2882 (23.2%) properties sold.
